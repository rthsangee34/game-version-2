Welcome to SHARP SHOOTER

Master the art of archery with your bare hands. Stay calm, aim precisely, and hit the bullseye!

🤚 + ✋

Step 1: Use Both Hands

Hold the BOW with your left hand. Your RIGHT hand pulls the string back to draw the arrow.

🏹

Step 2: Draw & Release

Pull your right hand back to draw the bow. When fully drawn, release by moving your hand forward quickly. The arrow fires toward the target!

🧘

Step 3: Stay Calm!

⚠️ KEY TRICK: If your BOW hand shakes or moves while aiming, your arrow will MISS. The steadier you are, the more accurate your shot!

🎯

Step 4: Hit the Bullseye!

You get 5 arrows. Aim for the center to score 10 points. Maximum possible score: 50. Good luck, archer!
